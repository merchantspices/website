About Us

Merchant Spices is a family business jointly owned and operated by Siddharth Tirodkar and Shweta Bhat Tirodkar.

When we first met in Adelaide many years ago, we both had a shared vision of starting our own business someday. Shweta's background in hospitality and her passion for cooking combined with Sid's business acumen and sharp Excel skills, we inaugurated Merchant spices in Feb, 2020.
